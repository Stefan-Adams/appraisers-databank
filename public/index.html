The purpose of ADB is to improve the quality of appraisal work everywhere, by providing you, the appraiser, with the best, unbiased comparable information available.

Welcome to ADB

ADB is a comparable information bank, where licensed appraisers just like you, can upload accurate property information gathered during their site visits. This information will then be available to other licensed appraisers. Only licensed appraisers will be able to access this information.

All the information you need

Just imagine, for the first time ever, you will have access to onsite information just like you were there:

  1. Sketches done by appraisers

  2. Non-MLS photos

  3. Room counts on all levels

  4. Lot size, contract dates, sale price, concessions

  5. Basement information

  6. Quality and condition

  7. Deck, patio, and porch sizes

Even better, you can make additional income from each report you upload!
